Event Info

A day on the courts. A win for the community.

Blazing Paddles returns in 2026 as a Round Rock Fire Foundation pickleball tournament built around community, family fun, friendly competition, and support for firefighter families.

Proceeds support Round Rock Fire Foundation programs — firefighter wellness, family assistance, crisis relief, and community outreach for the people who answer the call.

Volunteer

It takes a community to run a tournament.

Volunteers keep Blazing Paddles humming — check-in, court monitoring, food and music support, kids activities, and end-of-day breakdown. No pickleball experience required. Coffee, snacks, and a great team are guaranteed.

  • Player check-in & bracket support
  • Court monitoring and ball running
  • Raffle table support
  • Food, music & activity area help
  • Setup & tear-down crews
Volunteer with RRFF

Who shows up

Players from Round Rock, Georgetown, Austin and beyond. Firefighters, spouses, RRFF volunteers, local business owners, families, and community members all gathering for the same cause.

What you'll feel

Friendly competition, family-friendly energy throughout the day, and a real sense of community gratitude for first responders.
