Raffle Prizes

Prize donations are welcome anytime.

Raffle prizes help create tournament-day excitement and give community members another way to support firefighter families.

🎁

Donate Anytime

Businesses and community members may offer raffle-prize donations anytime before the tournament.

🔥

Prize Ideas

Sports gear, electronics, experiences, outdoor items, dining, signed memorabilia, gift baskets, and local business packages are all welcome ideas.

Prize donors may also sponsor the tournament if they want additional recognition and visibility with players and families.
